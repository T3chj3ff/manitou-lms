Why PDFs Are a Problem

PDFs are the 1 accessibility complaint for government websites. A PDF created by just "printing to PDF" is essentially a picture of text—completely unusable for screen readers.

The Golden Rule

**If you can avoid publishing a PDF, do it.**

Post content as a web page instead. PDFs should only be used for:

- Official forms that need signatures
